What is this?

This will show users/phones who are logged into a queue (dymanic queue, aka First Responder and Dispatcher), and if their phone is ringing, on a call currently, has someone on hold, or is ready to take a call.

Update

3/15/2019 - First responders were disapearing from the list, due to entering the dispatch queue. Added the option for multiple queues.

3/07/2019 - First Reponders are not "static", so they will always appear in this list.

Bugs

Some phones appear in the list multiple times after a live update from Asterisk, refresh the page and those entries should disappear.
